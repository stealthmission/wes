DRZ400 to KLX450 Headlight Conversion

Here are a couple conversions brackets for a DRZ400 to retrofit a KLX450 headlight assembly. Gone are the days of the outdated stock headlight. Now you can have a sleek, modern design that looks great and performs well. Pair with an LED bulb for added preformance and you're all set!

Custom FPV Airframe build and design

This is a custom FPV build that primarly focuses on the prop protection of a whoop while having the flight characteristics of an open prop copter. It started out with chopping up and frankensteining existing components and after many failed attempts has led to designing a custom airframe using Soildworks. Uses 1-2s lipos, 1002 14000-21000kv motors, 50mm props and 20A X12 boards.This is a work in progress and will be updated as it evolves.
