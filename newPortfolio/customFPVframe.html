Custom FPV Frame: Combining Protection with Performance

This custom FPV frame design represents a unique approach to solving the common challenge of balancing prop protection with flight performance. Through multiple iterations and extensive testing, we've developed a frame that offers the best of both worlds.

  • Integrated prop guards with minimal performance impact
  • Compatible with 1002 14000-21000kv motors
  • Optimized for 50mm props and 20A X12 boards

The frame was designed using Solidworks, allowing for precise optimization of weight distribution and structural integrity. Each component has been carefully considered to ensure maximum durability while maintaining excellent flight characteristics.

This project began as an experiment in combining different existing components and has evolved into a fully custom design. Through multiple iterations and real-world testing, we've refined the frame to provide exceptional protection while maintaining the responsive flight characteristics that pilots demand.
